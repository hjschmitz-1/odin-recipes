Banana Pudding Recipe

Description

This is a banana pudding recipe

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Pudding Mix
  • Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • Vanilla
  • Frozen whipped topping
  • Vanilla Wafers
  • Bananas

Steps

  1. Whisk the pudding mix into the milk
  2. Add the sweetened condensed milk and vanilla
  3. Fold in the frozen whipped topping
  4. Layer the mixture with the sliced bananas and nilla wafers
