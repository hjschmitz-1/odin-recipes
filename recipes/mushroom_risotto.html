Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is a mushroom risotto recipe

Ingredients

  • Broth
  • Olive Oil
  • Mushrooms
  • Shallots
  • Rice
  • Wine
  • Butter
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Chives

Steps

  1. Gather Ingredients
  2. Warm Broth
  3. Sautee mushrooms
  4. Brown shallots and rice
  5. Add wine
  6. Slowly cook rice
  7. Add in mushrooms, butter, cheese
  8. Season and serve immediately
